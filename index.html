FAQ

Why can you beat vendor prices?

Lean shop, direct vendor pricing, and efficient installs. You pay less; we still earn fairly.

Wrap vs paint?

Wraps protect OEM paint, are reversible, and cost less than quality respray—plus endless finishes.

How long does a full wrap take?

Typically 2–5 days depending on prep, panels, and film choice.

What’s the warranty?

Material up to 1 year; install 6 months (varies by film and job scope).
